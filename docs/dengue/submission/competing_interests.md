# Competing interests

The authors declare no competing interests.

None of the authors hold equity, consulting agreements, advisory-board positions, paid speaking engagements, or patent rights related to any of the three Phase 3 dengue vaccine programs (CYD-TDV / Sanofi, TAK-003 / Takeda, Butantan-DV / Instituto Butantan) discussed in this Perspective. None of the authors hold employment, equity, or licensing relationships with the commercial vendors of the immunoinformatics tools invoked by the pipeline (NetMHCpan, NetMHCIIpan, AlphaFold, DiscoTope, EpiDope, BepiPred-3.1, JessEV, Gurobi, IEDB, AFND).

The pipeline used in this work is a fork (https://github.com/JamesWeatherhead/immunoinformatics_platform-dengue) of the publicly released `immunoinformatics_platform` of Versiani, McCaffrey et al. 2026 *Sci Adv* 12:eaeb2066. Peter McCaffrey is a co-author both of that original publication and of the present Perspective; the fork extends his own previously published work rather than competing with it, and no commercial interest, royalty stream, or licensing arrangement attaches to either the upstream pipeline or the dengue fork. Both repositories are open-source under their respective licenses.

Cassia F. Estofolete and Maurício L. Nogueira are co-authors of the Estofolete et al. (2026) *npj Vaccines* paper on which the four-row composite framework rests. Their inclusion as co-authors of the present Perspective reflects the methodological dependence of this work on their prior framework, not a financial or proprietary interest in its further use.

Daniela Weiskopf has previously published on dengue T-cell immunology and IEDB-curated dengue epitopes; she has no commercial interest in the IEDB resource or in any of the vaccine programs discussed.

James Weatherhead and Nikos Vasilakis (UTMB) declare no competing interests, financial or non-financial, related to the subject matter of this Perspective.

No funding source had any role in the design of the study, the writing of the manuscript, or the decision to submit for publication.
