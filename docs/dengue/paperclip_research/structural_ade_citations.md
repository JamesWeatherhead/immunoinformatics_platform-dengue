- Year: 2003
- Volume: 100
- Issue: 12
- Pages: 6986-91
- DOI: 10.1073/pnas.0832193100

- Issue: 30
- Pages: 12479-84
- DOI: 10.1073/pnas.1106568108
- Vancouver: Chan KR, Zhang SL, Tan HC, Chan YK, Chow A, Lim AP, et al. Ligation of Fc gamma receptor IIB inhibits antibody-dependent enhancement of dengue virus infection. Proc Natl Acad Sci U S A. 2011;108(30):12479-84. doi:10.1073/pnas.1106568108. PMID:21746897.
